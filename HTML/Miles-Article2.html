How Dangerous is Carbon Dioxide?

By Miles Ehrhard | Opinion

March 22nd 2016 | 9:00am


The average American household products 7.5 tons of carbon dioxide a year and the average car produces six tons of carbon dioxide a year, America as a whole produces around a fourth of the world's carbon dioxide. Knowing this, the United States has vowed to cut CO2 emissions in half as well as China and the United Kingdom leaving only one percent for the rest of the world. Most people would agree that it is a step in the right direction but larger steps must be taken in order to keep the planet's greenhouse gases at a safe level.

The green house gas effect is the main contributor to global warming and the current crisis we are in involving the polar ice caps but environmental is not the only concern, the health of the average American is also at risk.
