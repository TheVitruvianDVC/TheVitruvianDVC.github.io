Peri Murray

Peri Murray is seventeen years old from Los Angeles California. She currently attends Da Vinci Communications High School, where she is the Co- Editor for the newspaper “The Vitruvian”.

Some of her interests include drawing, reading, playing her Wii U, and watching Adventure Time and Steven Universe. Her goal in life is to have her own animated show on Cartoon Network and be recognized for her art. Along with drawing she loves to write, which is why she joined alongside “The Vitruvian”.
